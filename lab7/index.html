AScCIMP

Associate of Science in Creative and Interactive Media Production

The programme aims to develop students' creativity, aesthetics, and innovative use of media, provide students with appropriate knowledge and skills in the development of interactive media systems and media production processes, and prepare them for further studies or lifelong learning.

Programme Intended Learning Outcome

Upon successful completion of this programme, students will be able to:

  • Relate creativity, aesthetics, and media technology to areas of media design and production;
  • Apply methodologies to develop interactive multimedia systems;
  • Appreciate major socio-political, cultural, and economic issues in media production;
  • Communicate effectively in a diversified workplace.
